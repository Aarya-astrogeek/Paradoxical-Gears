# Kinetic Mechanisms Suite

*A collection of experimentally engineered motion systems exploring geometric constraint, synchronized actuation, and unconventional mechanical interaction through advanced CAD modeling in Autodesk Fusion 360.*

---

## Overview

The **Kinetic Mechanisms Suite** is a repository of innovative CAD-based mechanical systems developed to explore how motion can be transformed, synchronized, constrained, and visually manipulated through compact engineered mechanisms.

This project combines principles of:

* Mechanical kinematics
* Constraint-driven motion
* Rotational control systems
* Linkage synchronization
* Parametric CAD modeling
* Geometric motion transformation

Instead of treating each mechanism as an isolated CAD exercise, the repository approaches design from a **system-level engineering perspective**, focusing on how geometry, interaction, and constraints collectively define mechanical behavior.

The suite currently includes three mechanisms:

1. **Paradoxical Gear Mechanism**
2. **Iris Slider Mechanism**
3. **Paw and Ratchet Mechanism**

All assemblies were designed and modeled in **Autodesk Fusion 360**.

# 1. Paradoxical Gear Mechanism

## Concept

The Paradoxical Gear Mechanism explores unconventional rotational interaction through linked curved rotating bodies that create a visually paradoxical motion effect.

Unlike traditional gear systems relying on standard involute profiles, this design investigates how motion can be transferred and perceived using non-standard geometry and synchronized linkage constraints.

## Engineering Focus

* Rotational synchronization
* Non-conventional gear interaction
* Linked motion systems
* Symmetry-driven geometry
* Motion visualization

## Key Features

* Curved gear-inspired rotating elements
* Compact dual-linkage assembly
* Parametric CAD structure
* Smooth constrained motion
* Visually engaging mechanical behavior

## Potential Applications

* Kinetic design demonstrations
* Mechanical motion studies
* Engineering visualization
* Conceptual mechanism prototyping
* Educational CAD showcases

# 2. Iris Slider Mechanism

## Concept

The Iris Slider Mechanism is inspired by the working principle of camera apertures and radial actuation systems.

The mechanism demonstrates synchronized sliding motion where multiple blades cooperate to create a dynamically changing central opening.

The project emphasizes precision constraint handling and coordinated motion control through compact mechanical design.

## Engineering Focus

* Radial transformation systems
* Sliding kinematics
* Coordinated motion constraints
* Multi-component synchronization
* Parametric assembly motion

## Key Features

* Multi-blade iris structure
* Smooth opening and closing motion
* Circular synchronized actuation
* Compact assembly configuration
* Motion-oriented CAD design

## Potential Applications

* Aperture mechanism demonstrations

* Mechanical synchronization visualization
* Kinetic engineering projects
* Precision motion experimentation

# 3. Paw and Ratchet Mechanism

## Concept

The Paw and Ratchet Mechanism explores controlled one-directional rotational motion using a locking and release interaction between a ratchet wheel and pawl.

The design demonstrates the engineering principles behind incremental rotational control systems widely used in tools, machinery, and motion-regulation applications.

## Engineering Focus

* Direction-controlled motion
* Incremental rotation systems
* Locking and engagement mechanics
* Rotational constraint behavior
* Functional mechanical interaction

## Key Features

* Functional ratchet tooth geometry
* Pawl locking mechanism
* Controlled rotational engagement
* Compact mechanical structure
* Parametric Fusion 360 assembly

## Potential Applications

* Mechanical transmission systems
* Tool mechanism studies
* Motion-locking demonstrations
* Industrial machine concepts
* Educational engineering visualization
